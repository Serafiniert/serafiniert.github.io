KptnCook

  • Cooking & Meal Planning App

    During my nearly five years at KptnCook, I contributed to nearly every part of the app. While my primary focus was on the iOS application, I also played a role in maintaining the iPad and Apple Watch apps, as well as a variety of iOS widgets.

    As part of our ongoing effort to modernize the codebase, we began transitioning from an imperative UIKit architecture to a more declarative approach using SwiftUI. This migration is still in progress but is now nearing completion.

    Below is a non-exhaustive list of key areas, features and 3rd-party-frameworks that I worked on:

    • Paywalls & subscription flows
    • Continuous Integration setup with Xcode Cloud
    • Onboarding & Offboarding
    • Premium features such as the Meal Planner, Discovery Search, and Fridge Finds
    • Migration of deep linking service from Branch to AppsFlyer
    • Networking and persistant storage
    • Analytics, attribution, and user tracking
    • A/B testing
    • Remote Notifications
    • …and much more
